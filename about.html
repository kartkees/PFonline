I’m a visual storyteller with a strong analytical side.

I studied Fashion & Branding at the Amsterdam Fashion Institute, where I also developed my skills as graphic designer. Whilst my previous job titles have been graphic designer I see myself as an allround creative; a visual storyteller.

I love learning and exploring new skills. I take my learnings wherever I can, for example through classes on Skillshare, Youtube or Creative Mornings. However, I'm not afraid to get my hands dirty by building and coding webpages, like the one you're currently on.
